who we are...

Established in 2022, "Yes, and Where?" is a collective of veteran improvisers dedicated to supporting aspiring improvisers in taking their skills to the next level. Before anything, we are friends who genuinely love working and playing together. We value honesty, transparency and playfulness. Whether you are looking to find a 101 class to start your journey or a seasoned improviser ready to shake it up with a new curriculum from across the nation, we are here to help you find the improv program that will best help you be the improviser of your dreams!

what we do...

Each member of our team is ready to help you find the best imoprov theater for your specific skill level and personal preferences of learning styles. We are here to build an improv journey that's right for you!

our mission...

"Yes, and Where?" is devoted to helping improvisers find safe, thriving community where performers of all levels can hone their craft and play an active role in growing world of improv. Our promise is to offer support and resources with authenticity and integrity.
